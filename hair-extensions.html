Types of Hair Extensions We Offer at Fusion Fame Hyderabad:

1. Clip-in Hair Extensions:
The most convenient and damage-free way to enhance hair length and volume. These are ideal for events and temporary makeovers. Clip-ins are lightweight, reusable, and can be custom-colored to match your existing hair without damaging your roots.

2. Tape-in Hair Extensions:
Semi-permanent extensions that last 6–8 weeks. These are applied using medical-grade tape and are great for everyday wear. They offer a natural blend and are heat and style friendly. Perfect for adding both length and density.

3. Micro Link (Micro Beads) Extensions:
No glue, heat, or braids required. These semi-permanent extensions last up to 3 months and are ideal for clients who want a long-term solution. Small metal beads are used to securely attach the extensions to your natural hair.

4. Sew-in Hair Extensions (Weaves):
Best suited for clients with thicker hair. Your natural hair is braided into cornrows, and the extensions are sewn into place. This method ensures a firm hold and lasts for 6–8 weeks with proper care.

At Fusion Fame, we use only the finest quality Remy human hair extensions, ensuring a natural finish with intact and aligned cuticles. This guarantees longer life, tangle resistance, and a glossy look.

Proper care and maintenance of your hair extensions will help them last longer. We guide you with a personalized aftercare routine, including shampooing tips, heat protection techniques, and styling recommendations.
