Hair extensions

Hair extensions are a popular way to add length, volume, and dimension to your hair without the commitment of a permanent change. Whether you're looking to try a new hairstyle, add length to short hair, or just want to switch up your look, hair extensions are a versatile option that can give you the results you want.

There are several types of hair extensions available, each with its own advantages and disadvantages. Here are some of the most popular options:

1. Clip-in Hair Extensions/Volumizers:
These are the most popular type of hair extensions that are used to increase the volume and length .These extensions are easy to apply and remove. They come in a variety of lengths and density, so you can easily find a set that matches your hair. Clip-in extensions can also be coloured to any colour of your choice so that your original hair will not be damaged due to excessive colouring.These extensions don't require any special tools or techniques to apply.

2. Tape-in Hair Extensions:
These are a semi-permanent option that lasts for 6-8 weeks before they need to be removed and reapplied. They're great for adding volume and length to your hair, and they're easy to style and maintain. Tape-in extensions are applied by sandwiching small sections of your natural hair between two pieces of tape, which holds the extension in place.

3. Micro Link Hair Extensions:
These are a semi-permanent option that lasts for 2-4 months before they need to be removed and reapplied. Micro link extensions are applied by attaching small sections of your natural hair to the extension using tiny metal links. They're great for adding length and volume to your hair, and they're easy to style and maintain.

4. Sew-in Hair Extensions:
These are a more permanent option that lasts for 6-8 weeks before they need to be removed and reapplied. Sew-in extensions are applied by braiding your natural hair into cornrows and then sewing the extensions onto the braids. They're great for adding length and volume to your hair, and they're easy to style and maintain.

When choosing hair extensions, it's important to consider the quality of the hair. Remy human hair is the highest quality option, as the hair cuticles are kept intact and aligned, giving the hair a natural look and feel.

Regardless of the type of hair extension you choose, it's important to properly care for them to ensure they last as long as possible. This includes washing and conditioning the hair regularly, using a heat protectant when styling with hot tools, and storing the extensions properly when not in use.

In conclusion, hair extensions are a great way to add length, volume, and dimension to your hair. With so many options available, you're sure to find a set that works for your needs and budget. Just be sure to choose high-quality hair and take proper care of your extensions to ensure they last as long as possible.
