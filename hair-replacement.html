Hair Replacement non surgical

Hair replacement is a non surgical hair restoration procedure which is the latest and most advanced method used among the non surgical hair restoration. In this procedure the hair unit are customised to match the client's existing hair colour, pattern and hair density so that the unit blends seamlessly, The Hair unit is then placed on the scalp using strong medically graded adhesives. These adhesives will last from 2-4 weeks depending on the clientâ€™s lifestyle and activity level. Once the adhesion wears down, it needs to be cleaned and reapplied. This maintenance will be repeated only when the adhesive wears down.

This nonsurgical method produces the best visible result among all the non surgical procedures, it is undetectable by sight and by touch to a certain degree. Hair replacement provides the flexibility to treat any area of baldness from small to big. People who have a major hair thinning or have been affected by baldness can choose this option as it does not damage your existing hair since the solution can be customised to a specific area. Hair replacement provides a convenient lifestyle solution as it need to be removed only when maintenance is required and it is comfortable to use while sleeping, taking a shower or another other activity that one would do when you had your own hair.

We are the experts in Non surgical Hair replacement, our custom hair replacement solutions are all hand made to order depending on the clients requirements. It can take any where from 1 week to 12 weeks to develop these hair systems depending on the area that needs to be covered. When choosing non surgical solutions it is always advisable to have a pair of unit, that can be used alternatively which would increase the life span of hair systems and also act as a backup in case one of the hair systems has to be repaired. Life expected from a pair of hair systems is 2-3 years depending on the care provided. In the norwood scale hair replacement is advisable for clients from stage 3 onwards to completely bald for men and stage 2 and above for women. Men with stage 2 baldness but with hair thinning can also go for hair replacement.
